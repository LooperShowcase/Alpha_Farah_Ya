Real Madrid CF

About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly known as Real Madrid, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. It was founded on 6 March 1902 as Madrid Football Club. The team has played its home matches at the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium since 1947.

Achievements

  • Most successful club in UEFA Champions League history, with 14 titles.
  • Numerous La Liga titles, Copa del Rey trophies, and other domestic honors.
  • FIFA Club of the 20th Century.

Famous Players

  • Alfredo Di Stéfano
  • Cristiano Ronaldo
  • Raúl González
  • Zinedine Zidane
  • Sergio Ramos

Club Colors and Crest

The club's traditional kit colors are all white. The crest features a crown and the initials "MCF" for Madrid Club de Fútbol.
